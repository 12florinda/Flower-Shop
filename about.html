about us

Why choose us ?

We are committed to offering only the finest and most beautiful floral arrangements and gifts, backed by service that is friendly, helpful and prompt. Our professional staff are dedicated to making your experience a pleasant one because all of our customers are important to us. We always go the extra mile to make your floral gift perfect.

Beautiful fresh original and creative flower arrangements are our proud and joy at Aangela’s Florist, roses, lilies, natives, reds, pinks, whites, yellows, bouquets, baskets, you name it, we create it, with delight..

Our florists have original, creative and fun ideas about how to make any gift occasion something special for both you and the recipient, whoever they may be and for whatever purpose your gift is for. We also offer a wedding bouquet and wedding function flowers service guaranteed to please.

flowers are more than just our business – they are our passion. Each petal, each bloom is chosen with care to ensure that our arrangements not only meet but exceed your expectations. Our goal is simple: to create distinctive, personalized flower arrangements that resonate with emotions. Whether it's a joyous occasion or a somber one, our creations aim to touch hearts, forge connections, and make moments unforgettable. Moreover, we believe that quality shouldn't come with an exorbitant price tag. Thus, while we offer premium floral goods, we ensure that they remain competitively priced, so every flower delivery in Quezon City from our shop offers both beauty and value.
